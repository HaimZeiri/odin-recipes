Scrambled Eggs

A delicious breakfast

This is a recipe for delicious, creamy eggs. A wonderful breakfast for any day.

Ingredients

  • Eggs (Duh)
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs in a bowl.
  2. Cook in butter over low heat.
  3. Swirl around the pan to make curds.
  4. Season to taste.

Tips

  • Use a non-stick pan.
  • Low and slow is the key when it comes to heat.
  • Remember carryover cooking. The eggs will continue to cook for a bit even after you take it off the heat.
