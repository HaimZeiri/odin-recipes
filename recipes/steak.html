Pan-seared steak with garlic butter

A delicious steak perfect for dinner. The garlic butter adds an extra level to it.

Ingredients:

  • Preferred steak (Personally: New York Strip)
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary

Instructions:

  1. Pat the steak dry.
  2. Season with salt and pepper generously.
  3. Preheat pan and add oil to it.
  4. Sear the steak on each side until nice brown crust.
  5. Add butter, rosemary and garlic cloves to pan.
  6. Tilt pan and spoon the garlic butter over steak.
  7. Continue cooking until desired doneness.
